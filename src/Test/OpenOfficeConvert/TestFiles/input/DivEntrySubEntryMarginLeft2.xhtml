waingˈwa.iŋn a kind of green vegetable from the coast, the vine is used for tying boats because it doesn't rot quickly in the sea.Waing og yiḳ jojeng nivesa ti sën hil ġeyeh maġa.Waing is a good kind of green vegetable which we cook and eat.
