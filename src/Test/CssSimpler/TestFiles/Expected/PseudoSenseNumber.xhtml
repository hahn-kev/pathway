ḳedik [qə.ˈⁿdik] N 1) fungus, toadstool-the generic term for edible fungi which grow on dead trees. Ḳedik ggol raḳ ḳele. Ḳedik grows on trees. (sem. domains: 1.5.4 - Moss, fungus, algae.) 2) ruffles-the the ruffles and frills on clothing are sometimes called by this name. Avëh dëkeping tob medëduu röpröp kwa los nyë ḳedik. Women cut clothes and sew ruffles around the edges.
