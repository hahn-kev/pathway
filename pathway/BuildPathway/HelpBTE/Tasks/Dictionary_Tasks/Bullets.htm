Bullets

If you want to show a bullet before each sense in a lexical entry, do the following:

  1. Display the appropriate list of stylesheets.

  2. In the Stylesheets pane, click the desired custom stylesheet.

  3. In the Stylesheet pane, click the Properties tab.

  4. In the Sense Layout field, select Bullet.

Important

  • In SIL FieldWorks, how the dictionary is configured affects bullets and their relation to other elements in the lexical entry.
