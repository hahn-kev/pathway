Pictures in text

For custom stylesheets, you can control whether pictures in your Scripture data are included in the output file.

  1. Display the appropriate list of stylesheets.

  2. In the Stylesheets pane, click the desired custom stylesheet.

  3. In the Stylesheet pane, click the Properties tab.

  4. In the Pictures field, select Yes to include pictures, or No to exclude pictures.

Tip

  • Normally, exported pictures are positioned to the left of the Scripture verse. You may be able to reposition pictures included in the output, such as in LibreOffice or OpenOffice. Some can use Prince to convert and edit the output file.
