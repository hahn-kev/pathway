Vertical Justify

When Paper is selected in the Navigation pane, the Properties tab shows the Vertical Justify field.

For a custom stylesheet, you can choose one of the following:

  • Top - aligns the top edge to the upper page margin.

  • Center - aligns the center of the object to the vertical center of the page.

  • Bottom - aligns the bottom edge to the lower page margin.
