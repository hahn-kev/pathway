Disable widow & orphan control

When Paper is selected in the Navigation pane, the Properties tab shows the Disable widow & orphan control field.

For a custom stylesheet:

  • If selected (), the columns will balance at the bottom even if just one line of a paragraph is present.

  • If cleared (), there will always be at least two lines of a paragraph at the bottom (or top) of a column.
