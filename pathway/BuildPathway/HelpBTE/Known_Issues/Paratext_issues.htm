Paratext Issues

When exporting to Pathway from Paratext, any introductory text that occurs before an introductory section heading marker \is# will be lost in the conversion process. To address this issue, place an introductory section heading marker \is# immediately after any major title lines.

Example

\h Luke

\toc1 The Gospel according to Luke

\toc2 Luke

\mt2 The Gospel

\mt3 according to

\mt1 LUKE

\is Introduction

\ip This is an introduction paragraph for \bk The Gospel according to LUKE\bk*.
