Charis fonts

  • Charis SIL and Charis SIL Compact fonts are used by the stylesheets.

    • Charis SIL is installed with SIL FieldWorks.

    • Charis SIL Compact font requires less vertical space between the lines for diacritics. If you don’t need the extra space, and you would like the lines to be printed closer together, Charis SIL Compact font might meet your needs.

Both fonts are available for free at http://scripts.sil.org/CharisSIL_download.

LibreOffice or OpenOffice

  • LibreOffice, version 3.2 or later opens *.odt files (OpenDocument format). Then you can also use the Pdf (Using LibreOffice) output option

  • If you open the *.odt file in OpenOffice, you need at least version 2.4.

Microsoft Word®

  • Choose the OpenOffice/LibreOffice destination. Open that file with LibreOffice or OpenOffice, and then export it in a Word format.
