Justified

When Paper is selected in the Navigation pane, the Properties tab shows the Justified field.

For a custom stylesheet, you can choose one of the following:

  • Yes -the text will be aligned at the trailing margin.

  • No - the trailing margin will be ragged (not aligned).

Note

Here trailing margin refers to the right margin for left-to-right writing systems, or the left margin for right-to-left writing systems.

In typography, when a line of text is justified, the spaces between words, and, to a lesser extent, between glyphs or letters, are stretched or compressed to make them align at both the left and right ends of each line of text.
