Select Your Organization dialog box

The Select Your Organization dialog box appears when you click the Defaults toolbar icon, or export data from a content-providing program, if have you not previously selected your organization.

After you have selected your organization, this dialog box will not appear anymore. Therefore, if you need to change your selection, do the following:

  • Close the Pathway Configuration Tool.

  • Press and hold the Shift key while you restart the Pathway Configuration Tool.
    Then it will appear when you click the Defaults toolbar icon.

Do the following in the Select Your Organization dialog box:

  1. Select your organization.

    • If your organization is not in the drop-down list, click Other to open the Organization Name dialog box.

  2. When your organization appears in the My organization button, click OK.

    The Set Defaults dialog box or the Export Through Pathway dialog box appears.

 Note

  • Copyright statements and other metadata are automatically set according to your selection here. You see this in the Publication Info tab (Set Defaults dialog box).
