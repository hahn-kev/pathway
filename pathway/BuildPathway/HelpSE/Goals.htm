Goals

  • Support multiple ”publishing roles” to effectively leverage the knowledge of publishing staff to help field teams

    • Good layouts (language/page size, and so on)

    • Knowledge about publisher constraints/layout constraints

  • Provide the field users destination options that are simple to choose

  • Empower the project manager to adjust output, if necessary

  • Acceptable defaults for pictures and headers

  • Support mixed left-to-right and right-to-left writing systems

  • Unicode and Graphite font technologies

  • Cross platform solution

  • Localizable User Interface (future)
