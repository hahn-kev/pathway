Shortcuts overview

Shortcut keys are individual keys and key combinations you can use to speed up your work.

 Note

  • Shortcuts also work in secondary and pop-up windows.

  • When you use a shortcut in the Navigation pane, you may lose focus in the Topic pane. To return to the Topic pane, press F6.
