Guide words

Guide words are positioned in page headers to allow the person using the document to find the desired page more quickly.

In a dictionary, the first and last headwords on the page are used. Consequently, all the words on the page will between them.

As the running header (head words), there can be different formats and differing amounts of information. To see examples, click a standard stylesheet, and then click Preview on the toolbar, or the button () in a dialog box.
