Snippet

A snippet is a stylesheet that covers one element or presentation concept, to specify, for example the number of columns. Another snippet will specify the page size, and yet another could specify colors.

The standard stylesheets that come with the Pathway Configuration Tool are made of more than one snippet. Snippets and other stylesheets cascade to give a predictable result.

If a change is made to one snippet, any stylesheet that use that snippet is also changed.

The standard stylesheet, called FieldWorks Styles, allows settings made in SIL FieldWorks to more directly affect the published product. Other stylesheets give FieldWorks settings less or no priority.
