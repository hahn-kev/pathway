-d ⁿd 1) N(inal) Possessive first person plural inclusive possessive suffix--this is the form used with the major class of inalienable possession nouns.
da ⁿda 1) N Sunday morning church service. lotu. Ya dëlë da dub ayo. They went to the service in the church. 2) N week. wik. Venë ya nedo Moresby da ti. His wife went and stayed a week in Moresby.
